CX-SCADA

Supervisory Control & Data Acquisition

CX-SCADA connects to PLCs, RTUs, and field devices across your plant, giving operators a real-time graphical overview of all processes with alarm management, trend analysis, and remote control.

  • Graphical process displays with drag-and-drop builder
  • Real-time and historical trend views
  • ISA-18.2 alarm management with suppression/shelving
  • Hot-standby redundancy for continuous operation
  • Built-in historian — no separate historian license
  • Web HMI: zero client install, any browser
  • Mobile alarm acknowledgment via iOS/Android
  • Report scheduler with PDF/Excel export
