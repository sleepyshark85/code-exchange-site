About Code Exchange

Founded by engineers who saw manufacturers struggling to find a single partner that understood both the technology and the factory floor — so we built one.

Who We Are

Built for Manufacturers, by Engineers

Code Exchange was founded in 2010 by a team of IT and automation engineers who had spent their careers working inside manufacturing plants. They kept seeing the same pattern: factories buying hardware from one vendor, fiber from another, SCADA from a third, and trying to integrate it all themselves — with no single party taking responsibility for the outcome.

We started as an IT trading company focused on the industrial sector. Over time, we expanded into telecom supply, factory automation, and eventually built our own industrial software products — CX-MES, CX-SCADA, and CX-IIoT Platform — because we couldn't find software that worked the way factories actually work.

Today, Code Exchange serves over 500 clients across Vietnam and Southeast Asia, with offices in Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, and Da Nang.
